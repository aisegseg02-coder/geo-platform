# Moharek Rebranding Summary

## Changes Made

### 1. Brand Identity
- **Name**: Changed from "GEO Platform" to "Moharek GEO Platform" (منصة محرك)
- **Tagline**: Updated to reflect Moharek's SEO expertise
- **Logo**: Changed from "GEO" to "محرك" (Moharek in Arabic)

### 2. Color Scheme
Updated from cyber-crystal theme to Moharek brand colors:
- **Primary Green**: #49A460 (Moharek's signature green)
- **Secondary Blue**: #4D86DA (Professional blue)
- **Accent Yellow**: #EABD2D (Highlight color)
- **Alert Red**: #FF0000 (Error/warning states)

- Gradient: `linear-gradient(135deg, #49A460 0%, #4D86DA 50%, #EABD2D 100%)`
- Updated all glow effects to use green (#49A460) instead of cyan

- Professional, clean typography hierarchy

- Professional, corporate aesthetic matching Moharek.com
